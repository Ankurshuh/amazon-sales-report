# Amazon Sales Service

## Project Title / Headline

**Amazon Sales Service Dashboard & Analytics System**

A complete data analytics project designed to analyze Amazon sales performance, customer behavior, product trends, and revenue insights using modern data analysis and visualization tools. The project helps businesses make data-driven decisions through interactive dashboards and actionable insights.

---

## Features / Highlights

### 📊 Sales Performance Analysis

* Analyze total sales, profit, and revenue trends
* Monthly, quarterly, and yearly sales tracking
* Region-wise and category-wise performance insights

### 🛒 Product Insights

* Top-selling products identification
* Product category performance analysis
* High-profit and low-performing product detection

### 👥 Customer Analytics

* Customer purchasing behavior analysis
* Repeat customer tracking
* Customer segmentation insights

### 🌍 Geographic Analysis

* State-wise and city-wise sales visualization
* Regional sales comparison
* Market expansion opportunity identification

### 📈 Interactive Dashboards

* Dynamic Power BI dashboards
* Filters for category, region, and time period
* KPI cards and trend visualizations

### ⚡ Business Intelligence

* Revenue forecasting support
* Profitability analysis
* Strategic decision-making insights
